Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web development, app development, and SEO services.

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and various CMS platforms. Our tech stack is chosen based on your project requirements to ensure optimal performance, scalability, and SEO-friendliness.

How long does it take to develop a website or mobile app?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms or mobile apps may take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation phase.

Do you provide SEO services along with web development?

Yes, we offer comprehensive SEO services including on-page optimization, keyword research, content strategy, and technical SEO. We ensure all websites we develop are SEO-optimized from the ground up to maximize organic visibility.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects and hourly rates. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote tailored to your needs.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages including security updates, performance optimization, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. Our 24/7 support ensures your digital assets remain secure and up-to-date.

Can you help with both frontend and backend development?

Yes, we are a full-stack development company. We handle both frontend (user interface) and backend (server, database, APIs) development, providing end-to-end solutions for complete web and mobile applications.
